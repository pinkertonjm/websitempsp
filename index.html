Recommendations For High School Athletes

Diet

Having a well balanced diet is important, because you want to keep your energy up, but not by eating junk.

Agility

Agility is important for most sports. Being agile is just being able to move quickly and in different directions.

Stamina

Stamina is important because if you don't have it you won't be able to perfrom that long.

Hand Eye coordination

Hand Eye coordination is very important for most sports because you need to be able to move your body and see where stuff is moving and what's going on. If Ronda Rousey had better hand eye coordination, she wouldn't have got kicked in the head.
